=== HOSTEL RULES ===
- No entry after 10 PM without prior permission from warden.
- ID card is mandatory at all times inside campus.
- Visitors are allowed only till 7 PM.
- Strict silence must be maintained after 11 PM.
- No cooking in hostel rooms.
- Ragging is strictly prohibited. Offenders will be expelled.
- Alcohol and drugs are strictly prohibited on campus.
- Electrical appliances (heaters, irons) are not allowed in rooms.
- Cleanliness of rooms is mandatory. Weekly inspection is conducted.
- Hostel fees must be paid by the 5th of every month.

=== LIBRARY ===
- Open from 8 AM to 10 PM on weekdays.
- Open from 9 AM to 6 PM on weekends.
- Digital resources and e-journals are available 24/7.
- Silence must be maintained at all times.
- Maximum 3 books can be issued at a time.
- Fine of Rs 5/day for late returns.
- Reference books cannot be issued; only reading inside library.
- Printing and scanning facility is available.

=== EXAMINATION SCHEDULE ===
- Mid Semester Exam 1: August (even sem) / February (odd sem)
- Mid Semester Exam 2: October (even sem) / March (odd sem)
- End Semester Exams: November (even sem) / April (odd sem)
- Supplementary Exams: June every year
- Results declared within 30 days of last exam.

=== PLACEMENTS ===

- Pre-placement training starts in 5th semester.
